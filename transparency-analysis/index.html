By changing the middle swatch in hue and value to be closer to either color 1 or color 2, the illusion of transparency can be conveyed. When the swatch is closer in hue and value to color 1, it appears as if color 1 is the transparent swatch on top. When the middle swatch is closer to color 2 in hue and value the opposite is true. And when the middle swatch is exactly halfway between hue and value of colors 1 and 2, neither color appears on top.
