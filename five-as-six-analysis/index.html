Similar to in the Four Colors Appear as Three interaction, the colors surrounding a color impact how it appears. In this interaction, when a color 50% between two colors in hue and value is surrounded by those two and then a border is placed between the two to further contrast the value differences, the original color can appear as two differnet ones.
